Our Technology

A Vital Sign Monitoring System

Durable and comfortable, the C3 is worn on your chest using electrodes and communicates live to your smartphone transmitting scientific metrics about your body - empowering you to monitor your health condition. The Cortrium cloud service allows your doctor or physician to remotely track your vital signs and take appropriate action.

C3 Metrics:

  • Electrocardiography (ECG II & III)
  • Heart Rate
  • Respiratory Rate
  • Skin Surface Temperature
  • Heart Rate Recovery
  • Sleep Analysis
  • Heart Rate Variability
  • Physical Activity Level
  • Body Position & Motion

Key Features:

  • Weight: ~ 30 grams
  • Battery: 72 hours when storing data on SD-card, 24 hours using live Bluetooth Smart
  • Bluetooth Smart
  • Automatic Synchronization with smart device
  • Memory: 2GB for 6 days concecutive recording

 

Cortrium Management Solutions:

Cortrium’s Wireless Monitoring System offers a management tool for healthcare professionals at hospitals or in pharmaceutical companies. Patients equipped with a C3 can be viewed on an iPad, in and outside of the hospital. Groups of patients can be viewed seamlessly at one time for a general overview and condition analysis. The access to present and past patient data allows health care professionals to make smart and quick decisions about their patients.

Product Description

Are you a researcher or are you building services which fully or in part rely on physiological or biometrical data such as heart rate & ECG, body temperature, respiratory rate, and physical activity?

The Cortrium C3 device is what you need. We deliver the tech platform; you build the services!

The C3 device is attached on the chest with any type of disposable standard electrodes. Once the C3 device is attached on the chest, the C3 device will seamlessly and continuously monitor electrocardiogram (ECG) at high sample rates.

Together with sensors for measuring respiratory rate (RR), body surface temperature, posture and physical activity (3-axis accelerometer). it opens for live monitoring of accurate heart rate variability (HRV), heart rate recovery (HRR).

Cortrium is working hard on the implementation of blood oxygen level (SpO2) and blood pressure measurements. Frequently asked questions

Medical device: The C3 device doesn't have the necessary CE-marking (class II) for being a medical device. It can't be used for medical purposes and doesn't substitute in any way a medical certified Electrocardiograph. It's a device that can record important life signals, in daily life, and to produce a continuous data feed.

CE-marking: The C3 device holds the CE-marking (class I)

Battery - recharge: The battery on the C3 device is rechargeable. You can use any Micro USB plug-in to recharge the C3 device.

Micro SD Memory Card: The Micro SD Memory Card can storage 2 GB of data corresponding to 24 hours of data upload from the C3 device.

ECG-signal quality and data streaming: ECG-signal quality is depending upon the placement of the C3 device relatively to the heart. Further, signal drop-out on 1 or more ECG-channels can occur due to expired electrodes (usually electrodes expire 7 days after opening of the packeting), a hairy chest, obesity and sweaty skin. Also the bluetooth connection can from time-to-time fall out - usually only for a few seconds.
